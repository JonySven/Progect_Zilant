Pricing Model

  • Fix price

    If you have limited budget, the work will be divided into two stages: requirements approval and development.

  • Dedicated team

    If you need to expand your development team for the limited period of time, we can provide you with engineers that will be working as a part of your team reporting directly to you.

  • Time&Material

    If you want more control over development priorities, Time&Material scheme of cooperation is the best fit. In this scenario you pay for the services delivered during the specific period and when the product is considered to be completed, the cooperation ends.
